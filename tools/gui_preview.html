Both panels render pixel-for-pixel from the firmware draw code. The Settings panel edits a value while the Main menu panel shows the live effect: wafer circle size (sized to the 150 mm max and cropped top/bottom), sweep extent (Back-Ctr vs Back-Frt), the park tick, and the red arm arrow. The generator runs only over the wafer — a pulsing ⚡ appears next to the arrow while it is energised. Use Tour shows to pick the demonstrated features.
